We will now jump over to SharePoint.
Open your SharePoint in a new browser tab.

  1. Navigate back to Microsoft365.com
  2. At the far top left, click on the App launcher (looks like a waffle)
  3. Find SharePoint and click on it
  4. At the top left in SharePoint, click on Create site
  5. Type: Communication
  6. Name: Training
  7. Click on Finish
  8. Once created, click on the Settings cog, upper right
  9. Click on Site information
  10. Below Hub site association, click on View all Settings
  11. Under the heading, Site Actions
  12. Click on Manage site features
  13. Locate, Metadata Navigation and Filtering
  14. Activate this feature
  15. Awesome! You can now use metadata for filtering for this site
  16. In the left nav, click on Home, which will take you back to the site
