On this Home Screen, we will be working with a few containers.

  1. In the Tree view, click on the Home Screen
  2. From the top toolbar, click on +Insert
  3. Under Layout, locate and select the Horizontal container
  4. Rename this: conHomeScreen
  5. Click on conHomeScreen
  6. Again, from the top toolbar, click on +Insert
  7. Under Layout, locate and select Container, not horizontal nor vertical
  8. Rename this: conIntro
  9. One more, Click on conHomeScreen
  10. Under Layout, locate and select Container, not horizontal nor vertical
  11. Rename this one: conCourses
  12. The container ordering schould be
    • conIntro
    • conCourses

  13. Let's update some properties, click on conHomeScreen

    Property Value
    X 0
    Y 0
    Width App.Width
    Height App.Height
