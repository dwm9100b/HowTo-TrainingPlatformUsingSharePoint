1. Go to: PowerApps
  2. First thing to do, change to a dev environment, upper right under Environment
  3. Then in the left nav, click on Apps
  4. Top toolbar, click on +New App
  5. Click on Canvas
  6. This will create a Canvas app from blank
  7. Give it a name: Training Platform
  8. Format: Tablet
  9. Press Create
  10. Go ahead and rename the default Screen1 to: Home Screen
  11. Before anything else, Let's make sure to do an initial save
  12. Upper toolbar icons towards the right, click on the Save icon
  13. Once its saved, we are ready to move on
